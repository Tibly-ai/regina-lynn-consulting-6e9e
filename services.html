What I Offer

Consulting, coaching, and speaking built for how your team actually sells.

Every business I work with has a sales process, a leadership bench, or an admissions funnel that could be performing better. I go in close, work with the people doing the job, and leave behind something that outlasts my visit.

01

Consulting

Sales and Admissions Systems That Hold Up Under Pressure

I work alongside your leadership team to look hard at your sales process, your admissions funnel, and the numbers behind both. We find where deals stall, where inquiries go quiet, and where your team needs better structure instead of more hustle. You walk away with a system built for your business, not a generic playbook.

  • – Pipeline and funnel audits
  • – Script and messaging refinement
  • – CRM and follow-up process design
  • – Performance benchmarks tied to revenue

02

Coaching

Leadership Coaching for Managers Who Are Ready to Grow

Some of the best salespeople struggle the moment they're handed a team to lead. I coach managers and directors one-on-one and in small groups, building skills that don't show up on a resume: giving feedback that lands, holding people accountable without micromanaging, and leading through a slow month without losing the room.

  • – Monthly one-on-one coaching sessions
  • – Team leadership workshops
  • – Difficult conversation practice
  • – Accountability frameworks that stick

03

Speaking

Keynotes and Workshops That Move a Room to Action

I speak at sales kickoffs, leadership retreats, and industry conferences on topics that change how people perform: closing with confidence, leading without a title, and turning admissions conversations into real enrollment. Every talk is built around your audience, your industry, and the results you want on the other side.

  • – Half-day and full-day workshops
  • – Conference and association keynotes
  • – Custom content built for your industry
  • – Take-home tools for immediate use
